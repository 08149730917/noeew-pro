Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and process.

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline depends on the complexity of your project. A basic website typically takes 2-3 weeks, while more complex projects can take 6-8 weeks or longer. We'll provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation.

What information do you need to start my project?

We'll need details about your business, target audience, design preferences, and any specific features you require. You can provide brand assets like logos, color schemes, and content, or we can help create these elements for you.

Do you offer website maintenance after launch?

Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance plans to keep your website secure, updated, and performing optimally. Our maintenance services include regular backups, software updates, security monitoring, and content updates.

Can you help with website content creation?

Absolutely! We offer copywriting services to create compelling content for your website. Our team can help with everything from page copy to blog posts, ensuring your messaging is clear, engaging, and optimized for SEO.

What platforms do you work with?

We primarily work with custom HTML/CSS/JS solutions, WordPress, and Shopify. However, we're experienced with various platforms and can recommend the best solution based on your specific needs and budget.

How do you handle revisions and feedback?

We include a set number of revision rounds in each package. You'll have the opportunity to review designs and provide feedback at key milestones. We value your input and will work closely with you to ensure the final product meets your expectations.

Still have questions?

Contact us directly and we'll be happy to help.
